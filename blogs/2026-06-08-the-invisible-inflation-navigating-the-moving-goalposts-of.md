The Shift from AI Performance to Predictability During the initial wave of enterprise AI adoption, strategic focus was almost exclusively centered on model quality and raw performance benchmarks. However, as the technology matures into the operational stack, the narrative has shifted toward the “moving goalposts” of consumption economics. For organizations seeking long-term stability, pricing predictability has become just as critical as the intelligence of the model itself.
