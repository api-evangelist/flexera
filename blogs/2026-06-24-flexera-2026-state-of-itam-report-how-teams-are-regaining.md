As organizations scale across SaaS, public cloud and AI, ITAM teams are being asked to do more than track assets: They’re expected to restore visibility, prove value and govern environments that are growing faster than policy can keep up. Based on a global survey of 512 professionals, Flexera’s 2026 State of ITAM Report shows clear progress. But complexity continues to outpace control.
