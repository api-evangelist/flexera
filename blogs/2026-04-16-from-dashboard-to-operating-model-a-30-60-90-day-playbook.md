Cloud sustainability cuts cost and carbon when it’s built into an operating model Sustainability is a compliance capability that’s become increasingly essential. Reporting requirements have expanded significantly over the past year, and they’re cascading through supply chains in ways that affect organizations regardless of where they’re headquartered. But it’s not enough to meet mandates like CSRD, CSDDD, CBAM in Europe and California’s SB 253 and SB 261.
